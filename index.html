About FGS

Falconhouse Grammar School, established in 1984 in Karachi, is now a chain of schools with 12 campuses in Karachi, and others located in Hyderabad and Multan as well. This is our 40th year of Academic Excellence. We are affiliated with University of Cambridge, London and Board of Secondary Education too. Most of our students opt to take the international GCE O Level examinations organized by the CIE of UCLES. We have approximately 6000+ students studying in Falconhouse Grammar School. Also, more than 3000 families are registered on our social networking pages and website. Falconhouse Grammar School (abbreviated as FGS) is one of the largest private English medium school systems in Pakistan established in 1984. It offers preschool, primary education, secondary education and preparation for local SSC and the international GCE. FGS has earned its outstanding reputation through its students' successes, its rigorous educational programs and its excellent and dedicated staff. It also promotes and encourages co-curricular activities in all of its campuses.

Our Vision

To impart standardized quality of education, while maintaining its affordability to the masses, in order to empower future generations and settle them in international mainstream.

Our Mission

To establish excellent scholastic standards, hence enabling our students, community and ourselves to become ambassadors of Islam in the light of Quran and Sunnah.

Our Values

Character and Integrity
Respect and Tolerance
Confidence and Curiosity
Responsibility and Accountability

In addition to our highly competitive academic programs, our co-curricular activities including athletics and performing arts program, are second to none. We are proud to be a comprehensive schooling system where every student has an opportunity to pursue his area of interest and excel in it. We have a kind, caring, highly-qualified staff who truly believes that each of our students can and will succeed.
Beyond the classroom, FGS offers its students many opportunities to reach the highest level of attainment and performance. Our students regularly participates in wide range of competitive and recreational sports events.
